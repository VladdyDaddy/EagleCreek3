About

Eagle Creek Lodge is conveniently located in Port Matilda, Pennsylvania, just 12 miles from Penn State University. We are situated in the center of the scenic Bald Eagle Valley on the banks of the Bald Eagle Creek. The lodge is designed to accommodate between 18 and 20 people and is fully equipped with a custom kitchen and bar as well as three surround sound televisions, making it the perfect space for entertaining large groups.

Whether you are looking to plan a relaxing weekend getaway with family and friends or attend a Penn State sporting event, Eagle Creek Lodge is the place for you. During football season, our cottage is a great place to spend the day before and after the game with ample space for all types of activities. Our cabin is also surrounded by popular outdoor activities including hunting, fishing and hiking. The nearby Scotia Range, Black Moshannon and Bald Eagle State Parks offer public game lands with trails, lakes and streams to enjoy year-round.
